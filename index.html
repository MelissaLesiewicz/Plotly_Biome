Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

We sampled over 150 individuals to see the different types of bacteria and the amount of each contained in their belly buttons. The dashboard below will allow participatants to enter their ID number and see the top ten types of bacteria that colonize their belly buttons and how many cultures were found for those samples. Participants can also compare themselves with others to see if the frequency of washing correlates to the number of cultures found.
